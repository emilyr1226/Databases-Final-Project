  • Students

    • Id: Auto Generated By Program
    • First Name: Max Length 100 Characters
    • Last Name: Max Length 100 Characters
    • Email: Valid Email Format, Must be unique throughout Students
    • High School: Max Length 200 Characters
    • Parent Income: WIP
    • GPA: Range(0.0 - 4.0)
    • ACT: Range(1 - 36)
    • Math SAT: Range(200 - 800)
    • English SAT: Range(200 - 800)
    • Guidance Councelor ID: Foreign Key, ID of existing Guidance Councelor

  • Guidance Councelors

    • Id: Auto Generated By Program
    • First Name: Max Length 100 Characters
    • Last Name: Max Length 100 Characters
    • Email: Valid Email Format, Must be unique throughout Guidance Councelors
    • Start Date: Must be valid date

  • Extracurriculars

    • Name: Max Length 100 Characters, Must be Unique
    • Type: Choice of (Sport, Art, Community Service, Career Development, Work, Other)

  • College

    • Id: Auto Generated By Program
    • Name: Max Length 200 Characters
    • Location: Max Length 200 Characters
    • Graduation Rate: Range(0.0 - 100)
    • Tuition: Must be Float
    • College Type: Choice of (Community College, Technical College, Vocational College, Public University, Private University)
    • Number Of Students: Minimum(1)
    • Room And Board: Must be Float
    • Email Of Contact: Valid Email Format, Must be unique throughout Students
    • Test Optional: Choice of (Yes, No)
    • Cost After Aid: Must be Float
    • GPA Average: Range(0.0 - 4.0)
    • ACT Average: Range(1.0 - 36)
    • Math SAT Average: Range(200 - 800)
    • English SAT Average: Range(200 - 800)

  • Participates In

    • Extracurricular Name: Foreign Key of existing extracurricular
    • Student Id: Foreign Key of existing student
    • Hours: Minimum(1)

  • Application

    • College Id: Foreign Key of existing college
    • Student Id: Foreign Key of existing student
    • Date Applied: Must be valid date
    • Date Received: Must be valid date
    • Application Type: Choice of (Early Decision I, Early Decision II, Early Action, Regular Decision)
    • Application Status: Choice of (Submitted, Admitted, Denied, Deferred)
    • Essay: Text Field, Max Length 1000 Characters
    • Scholarship: Integer
    • Visited Status: Choice of (Yes, No)
    • Interview: Choice of (Yes, No)

  • Supplementary Question

    • Question Id: Auto Generated By Program
    • College: Foreign Key of existing college, Unique with question
    • Question: Text Field, Unique with college

  • Supplementary Question Answer

    • Answer Id: Auto Generated By Program
    • Question: Foreign Key of existing question
    • Answer: Text Field
